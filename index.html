Privacy Policy — Store Listing

This privacy summary explains how we handle sensitive user and device data related to the app listing.

Why include a privacy policy?

Add a privacy policy to your store listing so users understand — clearly and transparently — how you collect, use, and protect sensitive data from users and devices. This helps build trust and ensures compliance with store rules and applicable laws.

What we consider sensitive data

Sensitive data may include, but is not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts and call logs
  • Camera, microphone, or other sensor data
  • Authentication tokens and device identifiers

How we use data

We use data only for the purposes disclosed in the app and in accordance with this policy, for example:

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To personalize user experience
  • For analytics and performance monitoring
  • When required by law or to protect the safety of users

Compliance & user rights

Users have rights regarding their personal data, including access, correction, and deletion where applicable. Describe how users can exercise these rights and provide contact details for privacy inquiries.
