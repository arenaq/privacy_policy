Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Wallet Jeremi handles your data.

Data Collection & Storage

Wallet Jeremi is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize the collection of user data:

  • Local Storage: Your username and password for the selected wallet service are stored locally on your device in SharedPreferences. This data is used solely to authenticate with the service and fetch your balance.
  • No External Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our own servers. Your credentials never leave your device except to be sent directly to the official wallet service provider you have selected.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics).

Permissions

The app requests only the necessary permissions to function:

  • Internet: Required to communicate with the wallet service and retrieve your current balance.
  • Notifications: Used only to alert you when your balance falls below the threshold you have set in the app settings.

How we use data

We use the data provided by you only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you with the selected wallet service.
  • To display your current wallet balance on the home screen widget.
  • To provide low balance notifications if enabled.

Compliance & User Rights

Since all sensitive data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can view, modify, or delete your credentials at any time through the App Settings. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.
